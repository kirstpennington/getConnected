    What are Connections?

    Connections

    GetConnected is a networking platform aimed at facilitating the building of relationships between like-minded individuals. So how do you do this? Well through connections, of course. Connections are other users of the GetConnected platform who have similar interests to you and who you can connect with.

    {% load staticfiles %}


    To find connections, navigate to the connections page through the navigation bar. Here you will see all the people you are connected with under the "My connections" tab (seen above). You can also find new connections on this page by clicking the "explore" tab. You can search through connections using the search bar seen on the left.


    Interacting with Connections

    Adding & Removing

    If you see someone you would be interested in connecting with, click the "Connect" button, seen on the right. Before you become connected with someone else, they have to first accept your connection request. If they accept, they will show up under "My Connections", you can view their profile and send them direct messages!


    {% load staticfiles %}



    {% load staticfiles %}


    If you no longer wish to be connected with someone, simply navigate to "my connections" and click "remove connection". The person will be removed from your connections and you will no longer be connected.

    If you are connected with someone and want to view their profile, you can click "view profile".

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Frequently Asked Questions

    1
    What do I do if someone keeps trying to add me as a connection and I do not accept?

    You can block other profiles from your profile so they will be unable to send you messages, connect with you or view your profile.

    2
    Is there a limit to the number of people I can connect with?

    No, you can connect with as many people as you wish.

    3
    How do I view more information about a person?

    Click on the person's profile in the connections page and a more detailed view will open.
