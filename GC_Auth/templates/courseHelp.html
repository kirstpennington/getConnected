Courses

You wouldn't be using GetConnected if you didn't love your GetSmarter experience! So why not use the courses page to find new courses, to keep your brain active... or recommend any courses you've already completed.



Viewing your Courses

Viewing your Courses

Viewing Courses

The GetConnected platform has a record of all the courses you have completed with GetSmarter. Which you can easily view by clicking "Courses" on the navigation bar. Be sure to select "My Courses" at the top of the page, to view only your completed courses ( see image on the right) If you select "All Courses", all courses offered by GetSmarter can be viewed

You can use the search bar to find a specific course you are looking for or the filter pane to filter by Subject, Institution or Recommendation

If you click on a course, you will be redirected to the GetSmarter website where you can read up additional information on that particular course.


Recommending Courses

Recommending Courses

If you have completed a course, you will have the opportunity to "like" or recommend it. All you need to do is click the heart icon see on the right.

Once this is clicked, it will show the heart filled in. You can always return at a later stage to unlike, simply by clicking on the heart again. The heart will then appear hollow. Courses which have many hearts, from many different users are "Highly recommended".

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

1
What happens if I do not pass a course?

If a course is not passed, it will not show up under "My Courses" and you will be unable to recommend it.

2
Is it possible for me to add my own courses to "My courses"?

No, your courses are added automatically once you have completed them.

3
How do I view more information about a course?

Click on the course's card and you will be redirected to GetSmarter's website to view more information.
