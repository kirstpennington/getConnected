Forums & Messaging

Create and use the GetConnected Forums to build your network. Attach articles, manage your privacy and direct message your connections to build lasting relationships.



Creating a Forum

Creating a forum

You can create a new forum at anytime and invite other people to participate!


Starting the forum

To make a forum, you can click on the plus icon on your home page (see right) or you can navigate to the forums page and click "create"

Once you click the plus icon, you will be taken to the "Create Forum" page (see below) where you can start creating and customising your forum!


{% load staticfiles %}


{% load staticfiles %}

On the Create Forum page you can specify the details of your forum and customise it to your preferences. This is your forum so you are able to choose who the participants are, the topics it will discuss and give it a profile picture. The list below gives more details:

1. Give your forum a name and description so participants know what its about

2. Select if you want your forum public or private - private forums can only be viewed by participants whereas public forums can be seen by anyone on GetConnected

3. Choose participants for your forum - participants will be able to add posts, image and partake in discussions

4. Each forum will also discuss certain information, topics or subjects. Here you can specify which subjects this forum relates to

Click Create to make the forum!



Finding a Forum

Finding a Forum

We know there are tons of Forums to look through, so we've made it easier for you to find what you want.


Searching & Filtering

If you want to see all the forums you are a particpant in you can click the "My Forums" tab.

Or, if you want to see all the forums on offer in GetConnected click the "All Forums" tab. This let's you find new forums to join!


{% load staticfiles %}


{% load staticfiles %}


You can use the filter pane on the left to filter forums by subject! This helps you to find forums that relate to your interests. Neat,right?

If you are looking for a specific forum, you could also use the search bar at the top, just type in the name and click search.



Participating in a Forum

Participating in a Forum

Once you are a member of a forum, you can participate in the discussions, like messages, reply and create new posts.

The image seen on the right is the area of the forums page where you get to make a post. You can add a title to your post, a message and even attach files or images. All forum participants will be able to see your post, like and comment on it. Any inappropriate posts will, however, be removed.


{% load staticfiles %}

{% load staticfiles %}


The item on the left is an example of a posted message. Here you can see you can reply, like and report other posts. Forums are a great way to stay in touch with collegues and classmates, while also finding new people with shared interests.

Direct Messaging

Direct Messaging

Besides messaging in forums, you can also directly message your connections! This is a great way to keep in contact with connections, plan a meet-up or even collaborate on projects.

Click the little message icon in your navigation bar, and select "view all" to start direct messaging people. The screen shown on the right should be seen.


{% load staticfiles %}

{% load staticfiles %}


Starting a Direct Message

Click "New Conversation" to select someone to message. The pop-up seen on the left should show up with a list of your connections. Select which one you want to chat to and tap "Message". You can also use the search bar to filter through all your connections.

Once you have chosen a person to message, simply type your message in the box where it says "Write your message" and tap send!




Group Messaging

You can also message a group of people at once! To do this, click "New Group" and select multiple people from the pop-up ( on the right). Everyone you select will be added to the group, and you can all start chatting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

1
What happens if a message is reported?

Messages can be reported by any participant in a forum if they feel the message is inappropriate. Should a message be reported it will then be inspected by a member of the GetConnected team and removed if it is infact inappropriate.

2
How can I add more people to a forum after creating it?

You can edit a forum's information after creation by using the edit forum page. Here you can add or remove participants at anytime.

3
Is there a limit to the number of forums I can create or be a member in?

No ways! Create as many forums and participate in as many forums as you like.
