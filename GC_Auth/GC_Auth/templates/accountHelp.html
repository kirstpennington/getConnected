Your Account

Your GetConnected account is an extension of yourself, your interests and achievements! Learn to manage your privacy settings and personalise you account to your unique style.


Personalising your Profile

Your profile is opened when you log in. This is how it appears to others on GetConnected.


Public & Private Accounts

If your account is public, anyone on the GetConnected platform will be able to view your account and all your details. If you account is private, only your connections can view your account.

Use the settings tog on the right hand side of your profile page to change this setting.

{% load staticfiles %}

{% load staticfiles %}

If your account is public, you can also use the settings tog to manage what information is shown to others. The settings page provides you with options to add or remove information visible on your profile. You can select which courses to show, which forums to show and what personal information is shown.





Profile and Header Pictures

This is your account, you need to make it your own and add some of your own style! Consider updating your profile picture to your latest selfie and adding an image to your header that really captures the essence of you.

Hover over your profile picture, click the button seen here on the right and follow the prompts to change or remove it.

{% load staticfiles %}

{% load staticfiles %}

Your header is the big strip of a picture behind your profile picture. Changing this really brings a unique element to your account and makes it more attractive to potential connections!

Hover over the header, click the button seen here on the left and follow the prompts to change or remove it.


Your information

By default your profile will show information about you which we have gathered from our GetSmarter databases. Information Shown includes :

- Full Name

- Number of Connections you have

- Number of Forums you are in & the names of these forums

- Your email address

- The Country you are from

- Courses completed at GetSmarter

You can update this information at any time as well as control what is visible on your profile. Click the settings tog and follow the prompts to do so.

Your Privacy

Here at GetConnected, your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. We have put measures in place to ensure your continued satisfaction. You, as the user, have the power to manage your account's level of privacy. You are able to control what is shown on your account, block other users and update their details in order to maintain your privacy. For more detailed information, please view our privacy policy .

Frequently Asked Questions

1
What happens when I delete my account?

GetConnected doesn't delete your account, we just deactivate it. If you email GetSmarter they will reactivate it for you.

2
Is it possible for me to set my profile picture to nothing?

Yes, when changing your profile picture select "default image" and your profile picture will become a default icon.

3
Where can I see all the badges available for me to unlock even if I have not earned them?

Unfortunately, badges are kept a secret until they are unlocked. Engage with GetConnected more to earn them!
