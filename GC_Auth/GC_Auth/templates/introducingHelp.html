Frequently Asked Questions

1
Am I required to engage on GetConnected in order to pass my GetSmarter Course?

No, GetConnected is for your own use. It is merely a tool to add value to your life and you are not required to use it.

2
Is anyone allowed to join GetConnected?

Only people who are completing or who have already completed courses with GetSmarter have access to GetConnected.

3
How much do I need to pay to use the platform?

GetConnected is provided free of charge to all students of GetSmarter. It is a life time membership!
